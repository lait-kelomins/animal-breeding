## Notes

### Food Usage in Breeding
The plugin uses these item IDs for breeding. Most animals have a "LovedItems" field in their NPC role that determines what food they accept.

### NPC Role Path
All NPC roles are located at: `Server/NPC/Roles/{Category}/{Subcategory}/{RoleName}.json`

### Item Path
All items are located at: `Server/Item/Items/{Category}/{ItemName}.json`

### Baby Variants
Livestock animals have dedicated baby NPC roles (e.g., Cow_Calf, Pig_Piglet). Other animals use scaling for babies.

### Dinosaurs
Currently identified dinosaurs:
- Raptor_Cave (Creature/Reptile)
- Rex_Cave (Creature/Reptile)
- Archaeopteryx (Avian/Raptor)
- Pterodactyl (Avian/Raptor)
- Tiger_Sabertooth (Creature/Mammal) - prehistoric but mammal category
